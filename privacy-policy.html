Privacy Policy — Bucket List by Burke Pin Scheduler

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Bucket List by Burke Pin Scheduler ("the app") is an internal tool used solely by the owners of the Bucket List by Burke Etsy store to schedule and publish pins to our own Pinterest business account.

Who uses this app

Only the store owners. The app has no third-party users, no sign-up, and no public interface. It connects to exactly one Pinterest account: ours.

What data the app accesses

  • Our own Pinterest boards and pins (to publish pins and read their status).
  • Analytics for our own pins (to improve future pin descriptions).

What the app does NOT do

  • It does not collect, store, or process any personal data of Pinterest users other than the account owner.
  • It does not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • It does not use cookies, tracking, or advertising identifiers.
  • It does not access direct messages, followers, or other accounts.

Data storage and security

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the owner's computer and are never transmitted anywhere except to Pinterest's official API endpoints over HTTPS. Pin content (titles, descriptions, product images) originates from our own Etsy store listings.

Data deletion

Revoking the app's access from Pinterest account settings immediately ends all data access. Locally stored tokens can be deleted at any time by the account owner.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact the store owner via the Bucket List by Burke Etsy shop.
